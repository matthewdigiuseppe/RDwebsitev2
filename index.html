Auditing Assessment for AI Vulnerability

An in-depth look at securing the value of your degrees.

Our audit process involves a comprehensive review of exams and assignments across an entire major program. We evaluate these assessments against current AI capabilities to determine the minimum grade a student could achieve using AI tools alone.

This "AI-floor" analysis provides a clear metric of vulnerability. By identifying where your assessments are susceptible, we help you strengthen them to ensure they measure true human understanding.

The Result: We provide institutions with credible validation of their degree's value. This certification serves as a powerful tool to recruit students who value authentic learning and to assure employers that your graduates possess verified, human skills.

Auditing AI Literacy in Curriculum

Preparing students for an AI-integrated future.

The Challenge: As AI reshapes industries, students require more than surface-level familiarity to succeed.

Our Approach: We evaluate the breadth and depth of AI literacy across your curriculum. We look for:

  • Technical Understanding: Do students understand the capabilities and limitations of Large Language Models?
  • Ethical Application: Are they taught to use AI responsibly and transparently?
  • Critical Analysis: Can they evaluate AI output for bias and accuracy?

The Outcome: A roadmap to integrate comprehensive AI literacy that empowers students to be responsible users and creators, ensuring they remain competitive in a tech-driven world.

About Us

Establishing a standard for the value of a degree.

REALdegrees was founded by educators concerned that AI challenges traditional academic integrity. As tools become more sophisticated, traditional assessments are losing their ability to verify human work.

Our mission is to preserve the human element in education. We partner with institutions to develop innovative frameworks that maintain academic rigor in a rapidly evolving landscape.

Our Goal: To create a "common benchmark" that allows students, employers, and educators to accurately assess the true value of a college degree. This clarity is essential in an era where AI challenges educational authenticity.

We combine expertise in assessment design, technology, and policy to create solutions for the challenges AI poses to education.

Our Philosophy

The core values that guide our approach to education in the age of AI

The Dual Challenge of AI in Education

Education is an investment in cultivated skills. Over-reliance on AI allows students to bypass the intellectual engagement necessary for competence. This erosion diminishes authentic personal growth and undercuts long-term societal advancement.

Degrees serve as signals of diligence and problem-solving ability. If AI becomes a crutch that inflates grades, the credibility of these signals is compromised. This challenges the labor market's ability to discern genuine merit from automated output.

Our Mission and Approach

Our organization is dedicated to the rigorous assessment of AI's role in education. We aim to ensure that emerging technologies are implemented in ways that:

  • Enhance, Not Replace: Advocating for AI tools that support understanding rather than substitute for intellectual engagement.
  • Preserve Integrity: Supporting policies that maintain the authenticity of credentials, ensuring they reflect human capability.
  • Reinforce Motivation: Upholding the value of incentives to ensure that the structures driving achievement remain intact.
